MYSPY

My Spy is a 2020 American spy comedy film directed by Peter Segal, written by Jon and Erich Hoeber, and starring Dave Bautista, Chloe Coleman, Kristen Schaal, Parisa Fitz-Henley, and Ken Jeong. The film follows a CIA agent who finds himself at the mercy of a precocious nine-year-old girl of a family that he and his tech support are surveilling while undercover.
